Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Final is a wallpaper app focused on curated artwork and a calm browsing experience. We keep privacy simple and collect as little information as possible.

Information We Collect

Final does not require an online account to use the core app experience.

  • Favorites, download history, and your nickname are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase status for Pro unlock is handled through Apple’s in-app purchase system.
  • We do not sell your data.

How We Use Information

  • To save your favorites and basic preferences locally.
  • To restore your one-time Pro purchase through Apple.
  • To respond if you contact us for support.

Third-Party Services

Payments and purchase restoration are provided through Apple. If you contact support by email, your email provider may also process that message.

Children’s Privacy

Final is not directed to children under the age required by applicable local law.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@finalwallpaper.com.
